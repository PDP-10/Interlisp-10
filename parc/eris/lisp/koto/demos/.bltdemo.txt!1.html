BLTDEMO.TXT

Most of you have probably seen the neat Smalltalk demo - the window with the
spinning star and the bouncing ring and box.  Well, now you can see it on your
Lisp system. Call (BOUNCE x y), where x and y are velocities in the x and y
directions respectively.  x and y default to 3 if omitted.  You will be
prompted to open a window.  The box shows whatever is near the cursor.
 Interesting recursive effects can be seen if you move the cursor near the box.
