MM1201 is a program that supports the Summagraphics MM1201 graphics tablet.  The tablet connects to your D0 via the printer port.  The program gives you easy access to all of the tablet''s many features and operating modes, and includes a simple Draw-type program that lets you use the tablet to paint on the screen.'
'
Use':  You need <Lisp>Library>RS232.DCOM.  Then load <Lispusers>MM1201.DCOM and call (MM1201).  This will bring up the main menu.  Documentation is on <Lispusers>MM1201.TED.  Comments, questions, etc. to me as usual.'
