'
Type in one quarter of a line of text, a newline, and one third of a line of text.  Select the second line and, using ``paragraph looks", justify it and indent the left margin by half of the page width.'
'
Select the second line of text.  Press backspace.  Move the caret to the end of the line and type in about three  (half) lines of text.  Move the caret to one third of the way from the left side of the first line.  Pressing return should cause the error.
