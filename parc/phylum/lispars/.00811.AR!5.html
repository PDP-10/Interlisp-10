It appears that the reason that you have had problems is because of a known bug -- you cannot move a sysout THAT HAS BEEN STARTED to another volume.  The current documentation on the "Remote Boot!" command says': "The sysout on the source volume must be ''untouched'', i.e., the source volume must never have been started."'
'
I have changed the subject of this AR to "Want to be able to "Remote Boot!" sysouts that have been started", Attn': JFung.pasa.'

All these are documented on the new 1108UG for Carol release. //JFung.
