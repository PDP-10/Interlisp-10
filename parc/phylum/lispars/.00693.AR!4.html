Larry Masinter has already answered about the withdrawal of the DLion local file system package (DLIONFS).  However, looking at your answer of 13 Apr 84, I have realized that this is not your problem at all.  In fact, I doubt that you are using DLIONFS at all.'
'
If you do not have DLIONFS loaded into Interlisp-D on a DLion, then opening a file on {DSK} will automatically create a "Core device" {DSK} in the virtual memory of your Interlisp-D.  Until recently, we had a problem where files on core devices disappeared between logouts, so this is why I suspect that this is what is happening to you.  New sysouts with a fix to this disappearing-files-on-logout  bug were recently announced to LispUsers↑.pa (ask your local Interlisp Liason if they have been transfered down there yet).'
'
To test my diagnosis, try the following, and tell me if my prediction is not correct':'
'
1) Have you loaded DLIONFS (test': (GETPROPLIST ''DLIONFS)#NIL)?  I predict no.'
2) Do any of the {DSK} files you create from within Interlisp actually appear after logout (using mesa tools) on your DLion local {DSK} volume?  I predict no.'
'
In spite of all of the misunderstandings, I think that your original complaint (FileBrowser not deleting files from {DSK} on DLion) is still valid.  I will keep you informed of the progress on this problem.'
